# Date

### When to use `Date` as a a field's `data type`

<p>
  Dates are very common to extract. Use `date` when you want to extract dates.
</p>

### How to annotate dates

When you annotate dates in your training documents, you will want your date input to **mirror** the document's date. **Type what you see.**

If you created your model before 30th April 2024, you must continue entering dates in a DD/MM/YYYY format instead of mirroring what is written in your document.
:::

### Validating dates

Dates are automatically formatted into ISO-format when parsed.

If a parsed date looks wrong, make sure that the date actually is wrong, and not a corrct date that has been ISO-formatted.
