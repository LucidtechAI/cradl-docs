{/* # Integrating Power Automate with Cradl AI */}
Power Automate is a popular automation platform. Cradl AI has a connector that you can easily integrate into your automation flows in Power Automate. 

:::info
## Prerequisites
- You need a CRADL AI Model. [Follow these steps if you do not have an AI Model.](/aiStudio/new-model)
- You need a Microsoft 365 account.
:::

## Import documents into Cradl AI from Power Automate
### Sign into Power Automate and create new Connector
Inside Power Automate: 
- Go to **My flows**
- Click **New flow**
- Click **Instant cloud flow**

You can give your flow a name and choose how to trigger the flow
- Choose a name. I.e: "My invoice flow". The name is only for your convenience. 
- Select **Manually trigger a flow**
- Click **Create** 
<div class="image-backdrop">
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/newFlowDashboard.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
- **Click on the «Manually trigger a flow»** box to add an input.
- **Select file** from the user input options.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/addInput.png)
</div>

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- **Click the + icon** below your «Manually trigger a flow» box.
- **Click Add an action** from the menu.
- Search for "cradl" and Select **Create document** from the available Connectors.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/createDocument.png)
</div>


### Create Connection to Cradl AI
<div  class="image-backdrop">
You will be prompted to Create Connection if this is your first time connecting Cradl AI to Power Automate.
- Give you connection a name.
- Log into your Cradl AI account in order to generate your credentials.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/createConnection.png)
</div>

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Inside your Flow overview in your Cradl AI account, **click Add trigger** and **select PowerAutomate**  
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/addTrigger.png)
</div>

### Create document
<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Back in Power Automate, paste your credentials into the Client Credentials field and click **Create new**. 
- You will prompted to create a new document. Give your document a name. 
- In the Body field, click the **lightning bolt icon**, click **See more**, and select **File content contentBytes** .
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/contentBytes.png)
</div>

### Parse document with human in the loop
<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Click the + icon below your Create document box to **Add an action**.
- Search for "cradl" again, and **select Parse document with human-in-the-loop** 
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/parseWithHitl.png)
</div>

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- From the Workflow dropdown, **Select your workflow** from the options.
- In the Document ID box, **click the lightning bolt icon** and **select body/documentId** from the options.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/parseWithHitl-2.png)
</div>


### Test your connection
- From the top navigation bar, **Save** your flow and click **Test**.
<div  class="image-backdrop">
- **Select Manually** and **click Test**.
- You will be shown your log in status. **Click Continue**
- **Upload a file** you would like to process and **click Run flow**.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/runFlow.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
- Head back to your Flow overview in Cradl AI.
- **Click Go to Validator**

    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/goToValidator.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
- Inside the Validator, **click Validate documents**.
- (If the validation status of your document has not updated, **try clicking Refresh**)
- **Click Validate documents**

    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/insideValidator.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
Congratulations, you should be seeing your first parsed document with the Power Automate integration up and running.

    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/validatedDocument.png)
</div>

## Export documents from Cradl AI into Power Automate
### Generate a webhook endpoint in Cradl AI
<div class="image-backdrop">
- Inside your Flow overview in your Cradl AI account, **click Add export destination** and **select Webhook**  
{/* - Search for "http" and **Select HTTP** from the In App connector. */}
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/exportToWebhook.png)
</div>

Inside Power Automate, repeat the steps to create a new flow: 
- Go to **My flows**
- Click **New flow**

<div class="image-backdrop">
 - Select **When an http request is received**
 - Click **Create** 
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/addTriggerHttp.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
- **Click on the Manual box**
- **Select Anyone** from the Who can Trigger The Flow dropdown menu.
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/httpConfigure.png)
- **Click the + icon** under the Manual box and **Add an action**
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
- Search for "terminate" and click it. 
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/terminate.png)
</div>

<div class="image-backdrop">
- **Save** your flow from the top navigation.
- **Click on manual** and **copy the HTTP URL**
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/copyHttpWebhook.png)
</div>

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Go back to Cradl AI and paste it into your Webhook endpoint uri. 
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/pasteEndpoint.png)
    - **click the Save button** in the top-right corner.
</div>

## Test your Webhook
- To test your Webhook export from Cradl AI, go back to the **Create document** with **Human-in-the-loop** parsing flow you created earlier in Power Automate. Trigger the import flow by **clicking Run** and upload a document to parse. 
- In Cradl AI, **click Validator** 
- **Click Validate documents** once the documents are processed.
- Inspect your documents and **click Validate**

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Go back to your Power Automate webhook flow. You will see your recent export in your run history. 
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/exportedToPA.png)
</div>

<div  class="image-backdrop">
- Click on a run job, and inspect it's output. 
Congratulations, you just parsed in Cradl AI and exported it as structured data into Power Automate!
<p class="image-backdrop-caption text-sm"></p>
    ![Image](../../static/img/powerAutomate/extracted.png)
</div>
